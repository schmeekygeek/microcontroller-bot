**STM32F10C8T6**
The STM32F10C8T6 is a popular member of the STM32F103xx medium-density peformance line family of microcontrollers that featurn a high-performance ARM cortex-M3 32-bit RISC core operating at a 72 Mhz frequency. 
**Features**
    **ARM 32-bit Cortex-M3 Core - 72 Mhz maximum frequency.**
    **Memories**
        64 or 128 kbytes of Flash memory
        20 Kbytes of SRAM
    **Clock**
        Internal 8 Mhz factory trimmed RC
        Internal $0 kHz RC - PLL for CPU clock
        32 kHz oscillator for RTC with calibration
    **Interfaces**
        Up to 9 communication interfaces - 2 x 12C interfaces (SMBus/PMBus)
        USB 2.0 full-speed interface
    **Timers**
        Three 16-bit timers with quadrature (incremental) encoder input
        16-bit motor control PWM timer with deadtime generation and emergency stop
