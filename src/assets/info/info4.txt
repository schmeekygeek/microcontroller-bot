**Attiny85**
Loved for their tiny form-factor, the Attiny series of microcontrollers are considered as the go-to microcontroller for projects where a small form factor is desired, and the number of GPIOs required is low.
**Features**
    6 General Purpose Input/Output (GPIO)
    Features a cpu of type RISC 8-bit AVR
    **Memories**
        8 KBytes of Program Memory
        512 Bytes Memory
        512 Bytes EEPROM
    **Clock**
        Maximum oscillator speed - Up to 20Mhz
    **Interfaces**
        SPI, I2C and USART communication protocols
        One Comparator
    **Timers**
        Two 8 bit Timers
