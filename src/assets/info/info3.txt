**PIC16F877A**
The PIC16F877A is one of the most popular 8-bit microcontroller in the PIC family of MCUs. While it's considered old by some and past its time, the PIC16F877A is no doubt, still extremely relevant even today
    **Features**
        Features a CMOS Flash-based 8-bit microcontroller that packs powerful PIC architecture into a 40 or 44 pin package
        **Memories**
            256 bytes of EEPROM data memory
            14Kbytes of program memory size
        **Clock**
            200 nanosecond instruction execution
        **Interfaces**
            Universal Asynchronous Receiver Transmitter
            3-wire Serial Peripheral Interface
        **Timers**
            2 high performance 8 bit digital timers
